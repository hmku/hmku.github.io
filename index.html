GoogleTranslate-Bot

GoogleTranslate-Bot is a Reddit bot written in Python that parses over ~1000 Reddit comments per minute for specific keywords and translates the comments. It then posts the translated result along with its pronunciation on Reddit as a reply to the original comment.

Alpha-Gomoku

AlphaGomoku is a neural-net based AI that uses Monte Carlo tree search and reinforcement learning to master the game of Gomoku, or five-in-a-row. It is modeled after DeepMind's AlphaGo Zero. This program was first conceived at Pure Storage's Hackathon12 with Charles Lien and Felix Wang.

So far, we've managed to train a beginner version of AlphaGomoku on ~300,000 self-played games, but our progress has been hindered by hardware limitations. We hope to continue refining and improving our model as time goes on.

Languages/Tools: Python, PyTorch
